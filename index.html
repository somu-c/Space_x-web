Rocket Architecture

Payload Fairing

Protects satellites and cargo during ascent. The composite fairing jettisons at approximately 3 minutes into flight.

Height: 13.1m Diameter: 5.2m

First Stage

Nine Merlin engines providing 7,607 kN of thrust at liftoff. Fully reusable with autonomous landing capability.

Engines: 9 Merlin Reusable: Yes

Second Stage

Single Merlin Vacuum engine optimized for space operations. Responsible for delivering payload to precise orbit.

Thrust: 981 kN Fuel: RP-1/LOX

Landing Legs

Four carbon fiber and aluminum honeycomb landing legs deploy for precision landing on drone ships or landing zones.

Material: Carbon Fiber Count: 4 Legs

Grid Fins

Titanium grid fins provide aerodynamic control during atmospheric reentry and landing approach.

Material: Titanium Function: Guidance
